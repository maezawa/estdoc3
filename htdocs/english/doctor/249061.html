"I want to maintain healthy beautiful teeth for life!” We are here in Ebisu offering our expertise to our patients with such expectations and desires. We strive to ensure highest accuracy and precision in our dental work. Unlike many medical treatments, taking medications doesn’t improve your dental conditions. It is the accurate and through treatments that will help and greatly affect your later years. Ebisu-Hiroo Dental Office is equipped with SurgiTel dental loupes that allow us to observe extremely small details that cannot be seen by the naked eyes, ultimately increasing the accuracy in our work. Dental caries and periodontal disease account for 90% of tooth loss. However, these two major dental diseases can be prevented nowadays. At Ebisu-Hiroo Dental Office, our dental specialists and hygienists work together as a team striving to provide a wide range of professional Preventive Dental Services. With our “Protection and Prevention” approach, we provide our community neighbors with dental treatments that do not involve the removal of healthy tooth structure by drilling or extraction unless otherwise necessary. We look forward to meeting you and helping you lead a long healthy life with a beautiful smile, with your own teeth.
