Dr. Sakai
One of our objectives is “to drill only when it is necessary while keeping the sensitivity to the minimum”. We respect your needs and preferences. If your choice is the natural color, we will select either hybrid or ceramic fillings. Depending your occlusion (bite) and dental conditions, we will suggest the use of metal fillings as more appropriate restorative materials. We offer a wide variety of treatments from Professional Mechanical Tooth-Cleaning (PMTC), Teeth whitening, Laser Dentistry and Dental Implant Treatments. We will provide you with full and complete explanations before we move forward and plan a best possible treatment for you. We ensure that your needs and desires, preferences are respected at all times and the best and the most appropriate treatment plan will be selected after consultations. Any questions you may have, please feel free to contact or visit us for professional answers and opinions.
